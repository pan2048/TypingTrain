dictionary

a dictionary, associative array, map, or symbol table, is an abstract data type composed of a collection of (key, value) pairs, such that each possible key appears at most once in the collection.
Operations | Meaning
----- | -----
Creation | the creation of the collection
Insert | the addition of a pair to the collection
Update | the modification of an existing pair
Retrieve | the lookup of a value associated with a particular key
Delete | the removal of a pair from the collection
Key existence | To check if the key exist in the collection
Iterate | To loop through the collection
